My Education Journey

I started school at the age of 2 years. At the beginning of my education, my parents had taken me to that school for daycare but I ended up learning with other students. The other students were having their end of term exams and I was also issued an exam paper. When the results came back, I was among the top students in the class and that is how I progressed to the next class and officially started my education in 2007.

Since then, I have undergone primary school and secondary school and currently I am undertaking tertiary education. I completed my primary school in 2016 after studying in various schools.I graduated with a Kenya Certificate of Primary Education and 340 marks After that I joined secondary school. Since joining, I studied in the same school till I did my national exam. I finished school in 2021 due to the covid 19 pandemic that pushed me 4 months into the next year. I graduated with a Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education and an average grade of B- with 57 points.

Schools Attended
